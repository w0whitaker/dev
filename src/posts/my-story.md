## What this is for.
To record in a very brief way the events and memories I have about my relationship to computers, and web development specifically.

### Early memories
#### 1. Pac-man
My dad is driving me to a birthday party at an arcade. He is talking about Pac-man, and tells me that he doesn't want me spending all my time at the party playing video games. I am 6-8 years old.
#### 2. Timex Sinclair
I am at a friend's house. They have recently gotten a [Timex/Sinclair](https://www.timexsinclair.com/computers/timex-sinclair-1000/) home computer. We are in the kitchen on an afternoon after school. I am afraid to enter anything because I am afraid I will break their brand-new computer. My friend tells me that there is "no way" I could break it by typing in the wrong thing. I will later learn that this is both true and not true.
##### Postcript.
In what I remember as making me feel somewhat ashamed, this Timex/Sinclair computer is given to my family when my friend's family gets a new computer.
