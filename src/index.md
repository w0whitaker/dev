# William Whitaker
UI/ UX/ Front-end developer

## `$ whoami_`
I'm a front-end developer and a [painter](https://williamwhitaker.art). As a developer, I'm focused on crafting delightful experiences for users - no matter who they are or how they're interacting with the site.

## `$ history_`
I've been making websites since 2009. Most of them have been portfolio sites for my artwork, and a few others along the way. I've had some experience with a lot of frameworks and platforms over the years, mostly Drupal, Ruby on Rails, and WordPress.

## `$ tail -f_`
Recent projects of mine include revamping a WordPress site for a non-profit, building an interactive pattern generator, and creating an inventory tracking application. More on the things I've worked on can be found on the '[projects]("/projects")' page.
